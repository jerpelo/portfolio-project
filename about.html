About Me

Hi I'm Jasmine!

I'm a future front-end developer based in Canada.
My programming journey began in October 2021 when I saw an Instagram post for a Python workshop - the back-end programming language which my husband was learning at the time. I thought I'd give it a try since I had someone to learn with but I have to admit that I struggled.
Then one day I saw another workshop-related post on Instagram but this time, it was for front-end development through SheCodes. I enrolled in the workshops immediately after sitting in on the introductory session and here I am today!
